    Terminologies

    List of all terminologies used across the site.

    Most of these terminologies should be familiar, and will be linking to external documentation for explanations. For terminologies that have stars (★), these may have slight different meaning and/or specific to this website that is not well defined elsewhere.

    Note: This may not align with general terms used by majority of players. I will try my best to align with others as site develops.

    Please refer to the basic definitions over from IIDX.org Beginner section, as this page will not cover the concepts (such as how to set Green Number/White Number, turning on FHS and switching between Normal/Classic Hi-Speed).

    Normal Hi-Speed

    The default Hi-Speed option that was introduced in IIDX Rootage. Uses value from 1-20 that has preset of Green Numbers.

    Classic Hi-Speed

    The old Hi-Speed option that was implemented since IIDX TRICORO. Uses values from 1.00 to 4.00, with increments of 0.5 from 1.00 to 2.00, then increments of 0.25 from 2.00 to 4.00.

    While it is mostly recommended to use Floating Hi-Speed in most cases, it is very important to understand how Classic Hi-Speed (and Hi-Speed Value) works.

    Floating Hi-Speed (FHS)

    The Hi-Speed option that (MOSTLY*) locks in your Green Number, regardless of your White Number.

    *: The mostly is mentioned here because if the song becomes too slow or too fast, the float may not set the GN to your desired number. This is discussed indepth in Another section.

    Note: In this site, it is implied that SUDDEN+ and/or LIFT is used with this option. The FHS without both SUDDEN+/LIFT behaves differently, and is not covered in this site.

    Hi-Speed Value

    The numerical value displayed in the Hi-Speed section of the gear. If Classic Hi-Speed is being used and floating Hi-Speed is off, this would be the Classic Hi-Speed value.

    Notes Time / Green Number (GN)

    The amount of time the notes are visible on screen. GN / (60 * 10) = amount of time, in seconds.

    i.e. 300GN = 0.5 seconds.

    Sub-Length / White Number (WN)

    The amount of area the SUDDEN+/LIFT covers. The total amount is up to 1000.

    SUDDEN+

    The lane cover that appears at the top. Has its own WN to go along with.

    Lift

    The option to lift/bring up the judge line. Has its own WN to go along with.

    Floating (Gear Change)

    The action of holding down the start button and turning the turntable.

    X buttons UP/DOWN ★

    This refers to holding down start and hitting the black keys (for UP) or the white keys (for DOWN) exactly X times.

    Each black key (UP) will add 0.5, and each white key (DOWN) will subtract 0.5 from Hi-Speed Value (in general).

    Start x4 ★

    Hitting the Start button 4 times in rapid manner (disables and enables Sudden+)

    x0.5 Reset ★

    Act of resetting the Hi-Speed to any multiple of x0.5 while floating Hi-Speed is on, regardless of current WN.

    FHS On/Off ★

    Turning on/off FHS by pressing Start + Effect.

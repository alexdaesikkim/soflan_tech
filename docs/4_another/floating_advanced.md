# Floating (Advanced)
The technique of floating, as covered in Normal section, is very easy. So you might be wondering, why is there an 'advanced' version.

## Problem with floating
While floating is easy and convenient, it has a core issue: Not every song can be conquered using floating only. Going back to A from pre-floating as example, a player who floats after BPM change happens will have to drop few notes. The reason why pre-floating is preferred for A is to minimize (if not eliminate) the notes dropped to change the GN.

For the majority of IIDX history (since Tricoro), players had to decide whether to pre-float or regular float for a song, and try to minimize the losses as much as possible.

That is, until people discovered (or rather, closely observed) how the game inputs work.
